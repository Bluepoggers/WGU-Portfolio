Project 02

Main Street Wash Laundromat Website

Web Design UX/UI Small Business

Designed and developed a comprehensive website for a local laundromat, focusing on accessibility, clear service information, and mobile-first design to serve the community's needs. The project involved understanding the diverse customer base, from busy professionals to seniors with varying levels of tech literacy, and creating an interface that works for everyone.

Design Approach

  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go customers
  • Clear pricing and service information architecture
  • Accessibility features for diverse customer base
  • Custom HTML/CSS with Squarespace integration
  • Easy-to-read typography and high contrast

Impact & Features

  • Improved customer access to business information
  • Professional online presence for small business
  • Easy-to-update content management system
  • Enhanced local community engagement
  • Location, hours, and contact information prominently displayed
Project 03

Youth Resources Mobile App (Concept)

UX/UI Design Mobile App Social Impact

Conceptual mobile application designed to help youth in need access essential resources including housing assistance, educational opportunities, and financial aid programs through an intuitive, accessible interface. This project demonstrates my commitment to using design for social good and creating solutions for vulnerable populations.

Design Goals

  • Simplify complex resource navigation for vulnerable populations
  • Create trust through clear, honest communication
  • Ensure accessibility for users with varying tech literacy
  • Provide offline access to critical information
  • Multi-language support for diverse communities

Key Features

  • Location-based resource finder
  • Simplified eligibility checker
  • Step-by-step application guidance
  • Emergency contact quick access
  • Privacy-focused design (no account required)
