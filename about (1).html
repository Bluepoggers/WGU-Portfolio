My Purpose

My career purpose is to apply UX/UI design to bridge creativity and technology, creating applications that are practical, intuitive, and effective for the people who rely on them. I aim to help cybersecurity organizations manage alerts and dashboards to reduce noise and support meaningful decision-making, while also developing solutions that make essential resources more accessible for youth in need.

My Journey

Navigating unexpected career transitions, including moving from cybersecurity work affected by funding uncertainties to business development, has fundamentally shaped my personal brand. This experience taught me resilience and the importance of transferable skills.

Rather than seeing my diverse background as fragmented, I learned to leverage it as a strategic advantage. The challenge of pivoting between technical and sales roles while pursuing my degree forced me to develop exceptional adaptability and strengthened my ability to communicate value across different contexts.

"This journey transformed my perspective from viewing career changes as setbacks to seeing them as opportunities to build a more versatile, valuable skill set that serves multiple professional paths."
